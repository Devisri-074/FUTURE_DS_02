# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer retention and churn patterns using customer subscription data. The objective of the analysis is to identify factors influencing customer churn, understand customer behavior, and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

The project was completed as part of the Data Science & Analytics Internship Task 2 at Future Interns.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze customer churn behavior
* Identify key factors affecting customer retention
* Understand customer lifetime and tenure trends
* Visualize churn patterns using dashboards
* Provide business recommendations to reduce customer churn

---

* Contract Type Analysis
* Internet Service Analysis
* Payment Method Analysis
* Business Insights
* Recommendations

---

## Key Business Insights

* Month-to-month customers show the highest churn rate.
* Fiber optic users have higher churn rates.
* Customers with longer tenure remain more loyal.
* Electronic check users churn more frequently.

---

## Recommendations

* Encourage long-term customer contracts.
* Improve service quality for fiber optic users.
* Offer loyalty rewards for new customers.
* Promote automatic payment methods.
